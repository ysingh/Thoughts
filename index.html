Thoughts

What is Thoughts?

Thoughts is a personal sentiment tracking & analysis application for Android, built on the idea that you may track your state of mind by providing text-input of what you're thinking. Examples of thoughts may include mini “tweet-like” blurbs about the your day, blog posts, or expressions of emotions like, “I don’t feel well” or “Life is good!”

Classify your thoughts!

The primary function of the application pertains to the use of sentiment analysis to classify user thoughts, which like posts in a blog, are simply text input by the user throughout time. As the application collects these thoughts from the user, it is able to analyze the user’s thoughts for positivity/negativity and affective/emotional state.
